Stir Fried Rice

Rice stir fried in a chicken base


Very flavourful! I added other vegetables - celery, red pepper - and it was very nice. It would have been better if I would have used a drier rice, mine was pretty wet and sticky…but that’s not the recipe’s fault LOL


Ingredients

  • Left over rice
  • Three Eggs
  • Carrots
  • Onions

Steps

  1. Fry Onions to golden brown
  2. Add beaten eggs
  3. Add chopped carrots and rice
